# Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Scenario Form browser extension ("Scenario Form", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the extension.

Scenario Form is a Chrome extension that helps users capture, save, manage, and replay reusable form-filling scenarios on web pages.

Your privacy is important to us. Scenario Form is designed to process form data locally in your browser and does not send saved scenarios, form values, page content, passwords, payment data, authentication data, cookies, or personal data to external servers.

## Information We Handle

Scenario Form may process the following information depending on how you use the extension and which forms you choose to capture.

### Form values

When you capture a scenario, Scenario Form may save values from form fields on the current page. These values are saved only when you choose to create or update a scenario.

Depending on the form, saved values may include:

- personal information, such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or addresses;
- authentication-related values, such as usernames, passwords, PINs, tokens, or similar fields;
- financial or payment-related values, such as card numbers, billing details, transaction data, or similar fields;
- other text or values entered into website forms.

Scenario Form does not require you to save any specific type of information. You control which pages you use the extension on and which scenarios you save.

### Website content and form structure

To detect and fill form fields, Scenario Form may read form-related content on the page where you use the extension. This may include field labels, input names, selectors, nearby text, and the structure of the form.

This information is used only to provide the extension’s capture, field detection, highlighting, rule testing, and filling functionality.

### Extension settings and rules

Scenario Form stores settings, custom field detection rules, presets, and user preferences in browser storage so the extension can work consistently between sessions.

### Import and export files

Scenario Form may allow you to export or import scenarios, rules, and settings. Exported files are created only when you choose to export data. You are responsible for storing and sharing exported files securely.

## How We Use Information

Scenario Form uses locally stored data only to provide its core functionality:

- capturing form values selected by the user;
- saving reusable form-filling scenarios;
- replaying saved scenarios on supported pages;
- detecting and highlighting form fields;
- matching scenarios and rules to pages or domains;
- testing field detection rules on user-selected tabs;
- storing extension settings and preferences;
- importing and exporting scenarios and rules.

Scenario Form does not use your saved form data for advertising, profiling, analytics, resale, or behavioral tracking.

## Local Storage

Scenario Form stores extension data locally in your browser using Chrome extension storage.

This may include:

- saved scenarios;
- saved form values;
- custom field detection rules;
- presets;
- extension settings;
- imported data.

We do not have access to the data stored in your browser by Scenario Form.

## Data Transmission

Scenario Form does not send saved scenarios, form values, page content, passwords, payment data, authentication data, cookies, or personal data to external servers.

The current version of Scenario Form does not use remote analytics, tracking pixels, advertising SDKs, telemetry services, cloud sync, or external AI services.

All executable code is included in the extension package. Scenario Form does not load or execute remote JavaScript code, CDN-hosted scripts, or dynamically downloaded code at runtime.

## Permissions

Scenario Form requires certain browser permissions to provide its functionality.

### activeTab

The activeTab permission is used to interact with the current tab only when you open the extension or start a capture, fill, or field detection action.

Scenario Form uses this access to detect form fields, highlight them, capture selected values, and fill saved scenarios on the page where you choose to use the extension.

### storage

The storage permission is used to save scenarios, field rules, presets, settings, and preferences in your browser.

### tabs

The tabs permission is used to let you choose an open browser tab when testing field detection rules. The extension may use tab URL and title information to match scenarios and rules to the correct page or domain.

Scenario Form does not collect or store your browsing history.

### Host permissions

Scenario Form needs access to web pages where you choose to use the extension in order to detect, capture, and fill form fields.

Because users may need Scenario Form on different websites, internal tools, admin panels, and web applications, the extension may request broad host access. This access is used only for the extension’s form capture, field detection, rule testing, highlighting, and filling functionality.

## Third-Party Services

The current version of Scenario Form does not send user data to third-party services.

Scenario Form may link to external pages such as documentation, support, changelog, privacy policy, or GitHub Issues. These pages are outside the extension and may be governed by their own privacy policies.

## Sensitive Information

Scenario Form can be used on many types of forms. Some forms may contain sensitive information.

You should avoid saving passwords, payment details, authentication codes, government IDs, medical information, or other sensitive data unless you understand and accept the risks of storing that information locally in your browser.

Scenario Form is not a password manager, payment vault, medical record system, or secure document storage service.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your saved scenarios, form values, page content, settings, or rules with third parties.

We do not use your data for advertising or cross-context behavioral tracking.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Scenario Form data remains in your browser until you delete it, reset extension data, uninstall the extension, or clear the relevant browser storage.

You can delete saved scenarios, rules, and settings from within the extension where supported, or by removing the extension from Chrome.

Exported files remain wherever you save them and must be deleted manually if you no longer need them.

## Security

Scenario Form stores data locally using Chrome extension storage. While this keeps your data on your device, no local storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

You are responsible for protecting access to your browser profile, computer, exported files, and any sensitive data you choose to save in Scenario Form.

## Children’s Privacy

Scenario Form is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.

Continued use of Scenario Form after changes to this policy means that you accept the updated policy.
